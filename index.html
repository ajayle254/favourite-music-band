About Wakadinali

Wakadinali, also known as Rong Rende or Zozanation, is a Kenyan rap music group based in Nairobi's Eastland area "Eastlando".

  • Wakadinali group, which was formed in 2003, has three members including Scar, Domani and SewerSydaa.
  • The group was founded by Scar and Domani.
  • Sewer joined the group later on.
  • The group has won several awards including Best Rap Group 2019, Best Album, Song and Collabo of the Year in 2019.
